<h2><a href="https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/cutted-segments1642/1">Maximize The Cut Segments</a></h2><h3>Difficulty Level : Difficulty: Medium</h3><hr><div class="problems_problem_content__Xm_eO"><p style="font-family: Nunito, Bangla416, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: Nunito, Bangla416, sans-serif;">Given an integer <strong style="font-family: &quot;Source Sans 3&quot;, Bangla416, sans-serif;">n</strong> denoting the Length of a line segment. You need to cut the line segment in such&nbsp;a way that the cut length of a line segment each time is either <strong style="font-family: &quot;Source Sans 3&quot;, Bangla416, sans-serif;">x</strong> , <strong style="font-family: &quot;Source Sans 3&quot;, Bangla416, sans-serif;">y</strong> or <strong style="font-family: &quot;Source Sans 3&quot;, Bangla416, sans-serif;">z</strong>. Here x, y, and z are integers.<br style="font-family: Nunito, Bangla416, sans-serif;">After performing&nbsp;all the cut operations, your<strong style="font-family: &quot;Source Sans 3&quot;, Bangla416, sans-serif;"> </strong>total number of cut segments must be maximum. Return the maximum number of cut segments possible.</span></p>
<p style="font-family: Nunito, Bangla416, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: Nunito, Bangla416, sans-serif;"><strong style="font-family: &quot;Source Sans 3&quot;, Bangla416, sans-serif;">Note</strong>:&nbsp;if no segment can be cut then return 0.</span></p>
<p style="font-family: Nunito, Bangla416, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: Nunito, Bangla416, sans-serif;"><strong style="font-family: &quot;Source Sans 3&quot;, Bangla416, sans-serif;">Examples:</strong></span></p>
<pre style="font-family: Nunito, Bangla416, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: Nunito, Bangla416, sans-serif;"><strong style="font-family: &quot;Source Sans 3&quot;, Bangla416, sans-serif;">Input: </strong>n = 4, x = 2, y = 1, z = 1
<strong style="font-family: &quot;Source Sans 3&quot;, Bangla416, sans-serif;">Output: </strong>4<strong style="font-family: &quot;Source Sans 3&quot;, Bangla416, sans-serif;">
Explanation: </strong>Total length is 4, and the cut lengths are 2, 1 and 1.&nbsp; We can make maximum 4 segments each of length 1.
</span></pre>
<pre style="font-family: Nunito, Bangla416, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: Nunito, Bangla416, sans-serif;"><strong style="font-family: &quot;Source Sans 3&quot;, Bangla416, sans-serif;">Input: </strong>n = 5, x = 5, y = 3, z = 2
<strong style="font-family: &quot;Source Sans 3&quot;, Bangla416, sans-serif;">Output: </strong>2<strong style="font-family: &quot;Source Sans 3&quot;, Bangla416, sans-serif;">
Explanation: </strong>Here total length is 5, and the cut lengths are 5, 3 and 2. We can make two segments of lengths 3 and 2.<br style="font-family: Nunito, Bangla416, sans-serif;"></span></pre>
<pre style="font-family: Nunito, Bangla416, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: Nunito, Bangla416, sans-serif;"><strong style="font-family: &quot;Source Sans 3&quot;, Bangla416, sans-serif;">Input: </strong>n = 7, x = 8, y = 9, z = 10
<strong style="font-family: &quot;Source Sans 3&quot;, Bangla416, sans-serif;">Output: </strong>0<strong style="font-family: &quot;Source Sans 3&quot;, Bangla416, sans-serif;">
Explanation: </strong>Here the total length is 7, and the cut lengths are 8, 9, and 10. We cannot cut the segment into lengths that fully utilize the segment, so the output is 0.</span></pre>
