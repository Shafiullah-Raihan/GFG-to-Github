<p style="font-family: Nunito, Bangla255, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: Nunito, Bangla255, sans-serif;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(39, 50, 57); font-family: Nunito, Bangla255, sans-serif; letter-spacing: 0.162px; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);">The&nbsp;</span><a style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; vertical-align: baseline; outline: none; display: inline; word-break: break-word; font-family: Nunito, Bangla255, sans-serif; letter-spacing: 0.162px; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);" href="https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/fibonacci-series/" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; vertical-align: baseline; font-family: Nunito, Bangla255, sans-serif;">Fibonacci sequence</span></a><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(39, 50, 57); font-family: Nunito, Bangla255, sans-serif; letter-spacing: 0.162px; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);">&nbsp;is a sequence where the next term is the sum of the previous two terms. The first two terms of the Fibonacci sequence are 0 followed by 1. The Fibonacci sequence: 0, 1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, 21</span></span></p>
<p style="font-family: Nunito, Bangla255, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: Nunito, Bangla255, sans-serif;">The Fibonacci sequence is defined as follows:</span></p>
<ul style="font-family: Nunito, Bangla255, sans-serif;">
<li style="font-family: Nunito, Bangla255, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: Nunito, Bangla255, sans-serif;">F(0) = 0</span></li>
<li style="font-family: Nunito, Bangla255, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: Nunito, Bangla255, sans-serif;">F(1) = 1</span></li>
<li style="font-family: Nunito, Bangla255, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: Nunito, Bangla255, sans-serif;">F(n) = F(n - 1) + F(n - 2) for n &gt; 1</span></li>
</ul>
